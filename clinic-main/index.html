Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept dental insurance?

expand_more

Yes, we accept most major PPO insurance plans. Our team will happily assist you in verifying your benefits and filing claims on your behalf to maximize your coverage.

How long does a typical checkup take?

expand_more

A comprehensive exam and cleaning usually takes about 60 minutes. New patient appointments may take slightly longer as we build your full dental history profile.

Is teeth whitening painful?

expand_more

Most patients experience little to no sensitivity with our professional whitening systems. We use desensitizing gels and customized trays to ensure your comfort throughout the process.
