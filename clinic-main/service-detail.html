Porcelain Veneers

Transform your smile with custom-crafted, ultra-thin porcelain shells designed for perfection and durability.

About the Procedure

Porcelain veneers are one of the most effective ways to achieve a complete smile makeover. These thin, custom-made shells are bonded to the front surface of your teeth to correct imperfections such as discoloration, chips, gaps, or misalignment.

At Premium Dental, we use the highest quality materials to ensure your veneers look naturally translucent and feel comfortable. Our digital smile design process allows you to preview your new smile before we even begin.

Process Timeline

Consultation & Design

We discuss your goals, take 3D scans, and create a digital preview of your new smile.

Preparation

A tiny amount of enamel is removed to accommodate the veneer, and temporary veneers are placed.

Placement

Your final custom veneers are bonded securely to your teeth, polished, and perfected.
